FSM Vending Machine

A digitally designed vending machine circuit coded in Verilog-A for CMP_SC 2270: Introduction to Logic Systems. The design implements a finite state machine driven by two T flip-flops, accepts quarters and dollars, tracks transaction state, and correctly dispenses product and change across three transaction scenarios. GTA V Traction Control Mod

A PC mod for Grand Theft Auto V that I built for fun and never published. Written in C# for ScriptHookVDotNet, it adds a dynamic traction control system that detects wheel slip in real time and adjusts for vehicle class, road surface (ice, snow, mud, wet concrete), weather, speed, and tire health. Includes hotkeys to toggle TC on/off and tune intervention strength on the fly. View report
